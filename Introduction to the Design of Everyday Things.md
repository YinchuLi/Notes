###评估和执行

七个问题：

- what does it means?
- what are my altematives?
- what happend?
- what do I want to accomplish?
- what can I do now?
- Is this OK? Have I accomplished my goal?
- How do I do it? 

同理心：能够理解和分享别人的感受。
